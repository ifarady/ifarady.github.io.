Work

I believe that collaboration helps to increase one’s capacity to approach more complex research problems. It gives a chance to work with a similar or multi-discipline interested scientific peer group around the world. My latest research and projects in computer vision applications are:

Vision Inspection for Industrial Project (2020 - now)

We conduct collaborative research with a multinational company in Taiwan to support the implementation of visual quality inspection. We build a new deep learning model and structure to solve the technical problems in real-world datasets.

  • Steel surface defect detection (fully-supervised learning)
  • Anomaly detection in steel and metal products (self-supervised learning)

Deep Learning in Medical Domain Research (2021 - Now)

We collaborate with a multi-field of medical doctors and practitioners to help them analyze the AD pattern based on MRI images.

  • Alzheimer’s disease image analysis from 3D and 2D data
  • Glioma tumor recognition with few-shot learning approaches

Research on Explainable AI or XAI (2022 - now)

It's a very challenging task and fun at the same time. We study how to make a human-level AI prediction become a human-friendly toy for customers. We work on designing tools and frameworks to help the user understand and interpret prediction may by our deep learning models.

Blog

Welcome to my world of thoughts, opinions, and travel adventures! I am thrilled to share with you my musings and experiences as I explore different places and cultures. This blog, isackfarady.wordpress.com, is a platform for me to express my opinions, share my travel stories, and offer insights that I hope will inspire and entertain you. Whether you're a fellow traveler or simply enjoy reading about new destinations, I invite you to join me on this journey. Get ready to be transported to different parts of the world through my words and images, and don't hesitate to share your thoughts and comments along the way.

about

Hey there! My name is Isack and I've got a background in telecom engineering and a Ph.D. in electrical engineering. I've spent over a decade working with data communication and project management. Currently, I'm a full-time researcher at the CVIT lab in YZU Taiwan. I'm passionate about image processing, computer vision, and using deep learning to analyze images and videos. I love learning and trying new things with creative people and communities. My focus these days is on sustainable development and eco-friendly technology. If you want to know more about my projects, feel free to drop me a message!
